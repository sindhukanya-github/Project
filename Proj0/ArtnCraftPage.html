My Favorite Art and Craft Work

My favourite hobby has never changed since I was young, and that is art. Ever since I was little I loved to create, craft and draw things. It has never stopped and even now, whenever I have a pencil at hand with my notebook or a piece of paper, I will create and draw. My favourite aspects of drawing, is creating characters of all sorts, inspired by people I see around me or pictures inspired by music I'm listening to.
